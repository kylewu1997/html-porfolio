Top 3 Movies According to Kyle

The top 3 movies according to me are:

The Dark Knight Rise

The Dark Knight Rises” is great because it delivers a powerful, emotional finale to Batman’s story, combining epic action, a high-stakes threat to Gotham, and Bruce Wayne's journey from broken recluse to redeemed hero

Chak De India!

Chak De! India is a gripping sports drama that's great because it blends a powerful redemption story with a smart, emotional look at women’s sports, teamwork, and national pride, led by a standout performance from Shah Rukh Khan

Avengers Endgame

The culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe's "Infinity Saga".
